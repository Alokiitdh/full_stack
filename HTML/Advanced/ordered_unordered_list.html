Poha Recepie

For Two People

  • 250gm Poha
  • 20gm sugar
  • 1/2 Lemon
  • 2 Green Chilli
  • 1/4th red chilli
  • Salt based on taste
  • 1 Potato of mediuym size
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Tomato
  • 1/2 table spoon mustered seeds
  • 3 table spoon peanut oil
  • 100gm roated Peanuts

Instructions

  1. Wash Poha with water
  2. Add oil in the pan
  3. Put musterd seeds
  4. Add Vegetables
  5. when the vegetables are fried add the masala
  6. friy till you get a nice smeell
  7. Now add lemon and sugar and do sottee
  8. Rins and add the Poha
  9. sotte till white poha become yellow and Serve
